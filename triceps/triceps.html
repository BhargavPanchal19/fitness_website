triceps Workout

Triceps exercises. Target the long, medial and lateral triceps heads.

Triceps Dips

Target: Triceps

Use a bench or chair, lower your body by bending elbows, then push back up.

Overhead Extension

Target: Long Head

Hold a dumbbell overhead with both hands, lower behind head, extend arms.

Triceps Pushdown

Target: All Tricep Heads

Use cable machine, elbows locked to sides, push bar down fully.

Diamond Push-ups

Target: Inner Chest & Triceps

Hands form a diamond under chest, keep elbows tight during push-up.

Triceps Kickbacks

Target: Lateral Head

Bend over, extend dumbbell back from elbow with control.

Close-Grip Bench Press

Target: Triceps & Chest

Lie down with narrow grip, lower bar slowly, push up explosively.

Bench Dips

Target: Triceps

Place hands behind on a bench, extend legs, lower body, and press up.
