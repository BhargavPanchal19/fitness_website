lowerback Workout

Lower back exercises. Learn how to increase lower back strength.

Superman

Target: Lower Back

Lie face down and lift your arms, chest, and legs off the ground simultaneously. Hold and lower.

Back Extensions

Target: Spinal Erectors

Use a hyperextension bench or floor to extend your torso upward using your lower back muscles.

Bird Dog

Target: Core & Lower Back

In a tabletop position, extend one arm and the opposite leg. Hold, then switch sides.

Good Mornings

Target: Hamstrings & Lower Back

With a barbell on your back, hinge at the hips while keeping the back straight, then rise back up.
