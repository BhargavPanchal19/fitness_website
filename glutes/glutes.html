glutes Workout

Glute exercises. Target your glutes with these movements.

Glute Bridge

Target: Glutes & Hamstrings

Lie on your back, lift your hips by squeezing your glutes. Hold and lower slowly.

Donkey Kicks

Target: Glute Maximus

On all fours, kick one leg up and back while keeping core tight.

Fire Hydrants

Target: Glute Medius

On all fours, lift your knee sideways while keeping hips stable.

Step Ups

Target: Glutes & Quads

Step onto a bench or box with one foot, drive up, then lower with control.

Bulgarian Split Squats

Target: Glutes & Quads

Elevate one foot behind you and lower into a deep squat. Push through front heel.

Sumo Squat

Target: Inner Thighs & Glutes

Take a wide stance, squat low, keeping chest up and knees outward.
