IT band Workout

IT band exercises. Build strength and stability in your IT band.

Standing IT Band Stretch

Target: IT Band & Hips

Cross one leg behind the other and lean sideways until you feel a stretch along the outer thigh.

Foam Rolling

Target: IT Band Release

Lie on your side with a foam roller under your outer thigh. Roll slowly to massage the IT band.

Side-Lying Leg Lifts

Target: Glutes & IT Band

Lie on one side and lift the top leg upward, keeping it straight. Engage your outer thigh.

Bridge + Abduction

Target: Glutes & IT Band

Do a glute bridge and push knees outward against a resistance band at the top.

Curtsy Lunge

Target: Glutes & IT Band

Step one leg diagonally behind the other into a lunge position, keeping your upper body upright.
