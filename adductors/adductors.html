Adductors Workout

Adductors exercises. Target your adductors with these movements.

Sumo Squat

Target: Inner Thighs, Glutes

Stand wide, toes out, squat down while keeping back straight and knees over toes.

Side Lunge

Target: Adductors, Glutes

Step to the side, bend one knee while keeping the other leg straight, return to center.

Copenhagen Plank

Target: Inner Thighs, Core

Support body on forearm and one inner thigh on a bench. Hold plank position.

Frog Pumps

Target: Inner Thighs & Glutes

Lie on your back, press soles of feet together, knees out, and thrust hips up and down.

Lying Leg Raises

Target: Adductors

Lie on your side, lift lower leg toward ceiling. Perform slow and controlled reps.

Banded Adduction

Target: Inner Thighs

Use a resistance band and pull one leg across the midline against tension.
