Neck Workout

Strengthen your neck muscles with these focused exercises.

Neck Flexion

Target: Front Neck

Sit or lie down, tuck your chin and bring your head forward towards your chest.

Neck Extension

Target: Back of Neck

While seated, tilt your head backward slowly, looking up towards the ceiling.

Lateral Neck Flexion

Target: Side Neck Muscles

Lean your head sideways towards your shoulder, keeping shoulders relaxed.

Neck Rotations

Target: Neck Mobility

Slowly turn your head from one side to the other, keeping chin parallel to the ground.

Isometric Neck Press

Target: Full Neck Strength

Place your hand on your forehead or side of the head, press your head against your hand without moving your neck.
