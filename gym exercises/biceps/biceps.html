Biceps Workout

Biceps exercises. Build sleeve-splitting guns with these exercises.

Biceps

Bicep Curl

Target: Biceps

Stand tall, curl weights up while keeping elbows close to your body. Lower slowly.

Hammer Curl

Target: Biceps & Forearms

Hold dumbbells with palms facing your body. Curl up and lower slowly.

Concentration Curl

Target: Biceps Peak

Seated, curl one dumbbell at a time with elbow rested on inner thigh.

EZ-Bar Curl

Target: Full Biceps

Use an EZ curl bar, keep elbows fixed and curl with control.

Incline Dumbbell Curl

Target: Long Head of Biceps

Lie back on incline bench, curl dumbbells while keeping arms fully extended at the bottom.

Preacher Curl

Target: Isolated Biceps

Using a preacher bench, curl weights with slow and controlled motion.
