Main Abs Routine

Crunches

Upper Abs 12*3

Lie on your back, knees bent. Lift your shoulder blades off the mat using your core. Don't pull your neck.

Leg Raises

Lower Abs 12*3

Lie on your back, hands under hips. Lift legs to 90Â°, then slowly lower without touching the floor.

Russian Twists

Obliques 12*3

Sit, lean back slightly, lift feet if possible. Twist torso left to right while holding hands together or a weight.

Bicycle Crunches

Upper Abs 12*3

Lie down, bring elbow to opposite knee, alternating sides. Keep the motion slow and controlled.

Reverse Crunches

Lower Abs 12*3

Lie on back, knees bent, lift hips off the floor toward chest using your lower abs.

Mountain Climbers

Full Core + Cardio

Get in plank position, drive knees to chest one at a time as fast as possible.

Side Plank

Obliques & Stabilizers

Elbow under shoulder, lift hips off ground. Keep body straight and tight.
