abductors Workout

Abductors exercises. Target your abductors with these movements.

Side-Lying Leg Raise

Target: Outer Thighs

Lie on your side, raise your top leg straight up, keeping hips stacked.

Clamshells

Target: Glute Medius

Lie on side, knees bent. Open top knee like a clamshell while feet stay together.

Fire Hydrant

Target: Hips & Glutes

On all fours, lift one knee out sideways, keeping hips square.

Standing Leg Abduction

Target: Hips & Outer Thighs

Stand tall and lift one leg sideways without leaning your body.

Resistance Band Abduction

Target: Outer Thighs

Use a resistance band just above your knees and step side to side slowly.

Lateral Band Walk

Target: Glute Medius

With a band around your thighs, squat slightly and take small steps sideways.

Curtsy Lunge

Target: Glutes & Hips

Step one leg behind the other and lower down like a curtsy. Alternate sides.
