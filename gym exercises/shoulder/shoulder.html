shoulder Workout

Shoulder exercises. Use these to build "coconut shoulders".

Overhead Press

Target: Front & Side Delts

Press dumbbells or barbell overhead from shoulder height. Lower under control and repeat.

Lateral Raise

Target: Side Delts

Raise dumbbells out to the sides with a slight bend in elbows. Pause at shoulder height, then lower.

Front Raise

Target: Front Delts

Lift dumbbells straight in front of you to shoulder level, then lower slowly. Maintain control.

Reverse Fly

Target: Rear Delts

Bend slightly forward at the waist, raise dumbbells out to sides focusing on squeezing the upper back.

Arnold Press

Target: Full Delts

Start with dumbbells in front of shoulders, palms facing you. Rotate wrists as you press up overhead.

Dumbbell Shrugs

Target: Traps

Hold dumbbells at sides and shrug your shoulders straight up, hold briefly, then lower.
