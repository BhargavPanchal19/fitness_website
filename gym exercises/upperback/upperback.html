upperback Workout

Upper bank exercises. Essential exercises for a thick upper back.

Bent Over Rows

Target: Lats & Rhomboids

Hinge at your hips, pull the weights toward your waist, squeezing shoulder blades together.

Face Pulls

Target: Rear Delts & Traps

Using a rope cable, pull towards your face, elbows high, keeping tension in the upper back.

Reverse Flys

Target: Rear Delts & Rhomboids

Bend forward and raise dumbbells to the side, squeezing shoulder blades together.

Pull Ups

Target: Lats & Upper Back

Grip bar shoulder-width and pull up until chin clears the bar, then lower slowly.
