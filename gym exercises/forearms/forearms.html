forearms Workout

Forearm exercises. Build "Popeye Forearms" with these exercises.

Wrist Curls

Target: Flexor Muscles

Sit holding dumbbells, palms up, curl only your wrists upward and lower.

Farmerâ€™s Carry

Target: Grip Strength

Hold heavy dumbbells and walk for time/distance to build grip and stability.

Plate Pinches

Target: Pinch Strength

Pinch two weight plates together and hold as long as possible.

Reverse Curls

Target: Brachioradialis

Use an overhand grip on the barbell and curl up to shoulder height.

Wrist Rotations

Target: Mobility & Strength

Hold light weights and rotate your wrists inward and outward slowly.

Wrist Roller

Target: Full Forearm

Use a wrist roller with weight, roll up and down to target all forearm muscles.
