quads Workout

Quads exercises. Build size and strength in this big muscle group.

Squats

Target: Quads & Glutes

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, bend knees and hips to lower down, then return to standing.

Forward Lunges

Target: Quads

Step forward with one leg and lower your hips until both knees are bent at 90 degrees. Push back up.

Leg Press

Target: Quads

Use a leg press machine to push weight upward using your legs, keeping feet flat on the platform.

Step Ups

Target: Quads & Glutes

Step onto a bench or box with one foot, press through heel to lift your body up, then step down.
