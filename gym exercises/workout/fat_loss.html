Fat Loss Workout

High-energy, calorie-burning exercises focused on fat reduction and stamina building.

Cardio Intervals

  • Jumping jacks, High knees, Burpees
  • Mountain climbers, Jump rope, Sprint intervals

Strength + Burn Circuit

  • Push-ups, Squats, Dumbbell thrusters
  • Lunges, Kettlebell swings, Plank to push-up

Fat Burning Tips

  • Keep rest between sets short (30–45 seconds)
  • Combine cardio and resistance for best results
  • Train at least 4–5 days a week
  • Hydrate well and get 7–8 hours of sleep

Fat Loss Nutrition

  • Focus on lean proteins: chicken, tofu, fish, eggs
  • Eat whole foods: fruits, veggies, oats, quinoa
  • Avoid sugary snacks and processed carbs
  • Drink green tea or black coffee before workouts (if tolerated)
