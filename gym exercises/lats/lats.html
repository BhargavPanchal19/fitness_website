lats Workout

Lats exercises. Develop back width with these movements.

Pull-Ups

Target: Upper Lats

Hang from a bar and pull your body up until your chin passes the bar, then lower down slowly.

Lat Pulldown

Target: Lats

Sit at a lat pulldown machine and pull the bar down toward your chest with a wide grip.

Seated Cable Row

Target: Mid & Lower Lats

Sit upright and pull the handle toward your torso, squeezing shoulder blades together.

One-Arm Dumbbell Row

Target: Lats & Rear Delts

Place one knee on a bench, row the dumbbell up toward your hip while keeping your back flat.

Straight-Arm Pulldown

Target: Lower Lats

Use a cable machine and pull the bar downward with straight arms to your thighs, then return slowly.
