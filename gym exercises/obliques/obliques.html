upperback Workout

Upper bank exercises. Essential exercises for a thick upper back.

Russian Twists

Target: Obliques

Sit with feet lifted, twist your torso left and right holding a weight or medicine ball.

Side Plank Dips

Target: Side Core Muscles

Start in side plank, dip hips toward the floor and raise back up slowly.

Standing Side Crunch

Target: Obliques

Raise one knee and crunch to the side with elbow meeting it, alternating sides.

Bicycle Crunches

Target: Obliques & Core

Alternate elbow to opposite knee in a pedaling motion while lying down.
