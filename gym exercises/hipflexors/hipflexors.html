Hipflexors Workout

Hip flexor exercises. Target your hip flexors with these exercises.

Lunge Stretch

Target: Hip Flexors

Step forward into a deep lunge, drop the back knee, and press hips forward to stretch.

Straight Leg Raises

Target: Hip Flexors & Core

Lie on your back and slowly lift your legs up, keeping them straight. Lower down with control.

Mountain Climbers

Target: Hip Flexors & Core

In plank position, rapidly drive your knees toward your chest one at a time.

Hip Flexor March

Target: Hip Flexors

Stand tall and alternate lifting each knee toward your chest with control.

Band Leg Raises

Target: Hip Flexors

Attach a band to your ankle and slowly raise the leg forward against resistance.
