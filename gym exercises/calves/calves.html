calves Workout

Calf exercises. Essential exercises for developing calf muscle size.

Standing Calf Raise

Target: Gastrocnemius

Stand tall, raise heels off the floor, hold at the top, and lower slowly.

Seated Calf Raise

Target: Soleus

Sit on a bench with weight on knees, raise heels, pause, and lower slowly.

Single-Leg Calf Raise

Target: Balance & Strength

Stand on one foot, raise your heel slowly, hold, then lower with control.

Donkey Calf Raise

Target: Deep Stretch

Bend forward holding support, raise your heels while maintaining a stretch.

Resistance Band Calf Flex

Target: Controlled Strength

Wrap band on foot, flex and point the toes against resistance.

Jump Rope

Target: Endurance

Jump on toes repeatedly with rhythm to strengthen calves and stamina.
