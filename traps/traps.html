Traps Workout

Build bigger, stronger traps with these effective movements.

Barbell Shrugs

Target: Upper Traps

Hold a barbell and shrug your shoulders straight up. Hold briefly, then lower slowly.

Dumbbell Shrugs

Target: Upper Traps

Hold dumbbells at your sides and shrug. Maintain good posture and avoid rolling shoulders.

Face Pulls

Target: Mid & Lower Traps

Use a cable machine or band. Pull towards your forehead, keeping elbows high.

T-Bar Rows

Target: Middle Traps

Row the barbell toward your chest using a wide grip to emphasize trap activation.

Incline Dumbbell Shrugs

Target: Lower Traps

Lie chest-down on an incline bench and shrug upward to isolate the lower traps.

Farmer’s Walk

Target: Full Traps

Grab heavy dumbbells and walk slowly. Focus on posture and trap engagement.
