Cardio Workouts

Boost your heart health, burn calories, and improve endurance with these cardio workouts.

Indoor Cardio

  • Jumping jacks, High knees, Butt kicks
  • Mountain climbers, Burpees, Skater jumps

Outdoor Cardio

  • Running or Jogging, Brisk walking, Cycling
  • Hill sprints, Stair climbs, Jump rope

Low-Impact Options

  • Step touch, March in place, Arm swings
  • Elliptical, Swimming, Dancing

Tips for Effective Cardio

  • Warm up before and cool down after your session
  • Mix steady-state and interval workouts
  • Stay hydrated and track your heart rate
  • Aim for 150–300 minutes of cardio per week
