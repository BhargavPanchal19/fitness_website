Muscle Building Workout

Gain size and strength with proven hypertrophy training plans.

Upper Body Mass

  • Barbell bench press, Incline dumbbell press
  • Pull-ups, Lat pulldown, Bent-over rows
  • Military press, Dumbbell lateral raises

Leg Day Growth

  • Barbell back squats, Leg press, Walking lunges
  • Romanian deadlifts, Calf raises, Glute bridges

Arm & Core Training

  • EZ bar curls, Hammer curls, Tricep dips
  • Russian twists, Hanging leg raises, Weighted planks

Muscle Building Tips

  • Train each muscle group twice a week
  • Focus on progressive overload (more reps/weight weekly)
  • Rest 60–90 seconds between sets for muscle recovery
  • Sleep 7–8 hours to maximize muscle repair
  • Eat in a slight calorie surplus with high protein intake
